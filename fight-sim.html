Random Dungeon Keys

Keys generate dungeons with varying difficulty and monster layouts. The key determines:

  • Front (0-2): Difficulty tier - Courage (skip 2), Judgment (skip 3), Wisdom (skip 4)
  • Behind (0-3): Location - Canyon, Fortress, Mausoleum, Ancient Citadel
  • MaxFloor (10-24): Total floors
  • FloorTime (5-20): Minutes per floor
  • StartNum (1-5): Starting monster position offset

For optimal farming, look for keys with MaxFloor=24 and FloorTime=20.

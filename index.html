Whipper - Idle RPG

An idle RPG game for those who are busy

Send your adventurer out to explore a dungeon. Wait for them to return with loot.

After returning from a dungeon, the adventurer's level will revert to 1! Therefore, to conquer difficult dungeons, it is essential to upgrade your equipment!

If you have any comments or suggestions for this app, contact me though email or Twitter DM.

The in-game text is machine translated (DeepL).

If you find any errors in the translation, or would like to help translate, please contact me.

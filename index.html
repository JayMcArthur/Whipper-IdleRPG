About Whipper

Whipper - Idle RPG is an idle dungeon crawler game for mobile devices. Send your adventurer into dungeons to battle monsters and collect loot. After each expedition, your adventurer's level resets to 1, so upgrading your equipment is key to conquering harder dungeons!
